About:

Desi Charades is an Indian version of the Dumb Charades, where players act out popular Indian film names without speaking. Players have a limited time to guess the film names and earn points. Each team takes turns selecting an actor who silently acts out the film name. If the team guesses correctly within the time limit, they earn points.
